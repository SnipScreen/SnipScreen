Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2025

Introduction

This policy explains how the SnipScreen Chrome Extension ("Extension", "we", "us", "our") handles information when you use it. We respect your privacy.

Core Principle: Local Processing Only

  • SnipScreen works entirely on your computer within the Chrome browser.
  • No data is sent to external servers. Your screenshots and edits stay local.

Information Handled (Locally)

SnipScreen interacts with the following data, keeping it on your machine:

  • Screenshot Data: Images captured are stored temporarily in browser storage (chrome.storage.local) only while the editor is open.
  • User Settings: Your preferred save folder name is kept in Chrome's sync storage (chrome.storage.sync) for convenience.

How Information Is Used

We use this local data strictly for these Extension functions:

  • Displaying the captured screenshot in the editor tab.
  • Enabling editing tools (crop, annotate, etc.) to modify the displayed image.
  • Executing the "Copy to Clipboard" and "Save as PNG" actions based on your clicks.
  • Applying your saved setting for the default filename/path when saving.

Data Storage & Retention

  • Screenshot data is temporary and automatically deleted when you close the SnipScreen editor tab.
  • Settings data remains until you change it, remove the extension, or clear Chrome's synced data.
  • We rely on Google Chrome's security mechanisms for protecting this locally stored information.

Data Sharing: Strictly None

We emphasize that SnipScreen does not share any of your data with us or any third parties.

  • The "Copy" feature interacts only with your computer's clipboard, initiated by you.
  • The "Save" feature interacts only with your computer's file system via the browser's download API, initiated by you.

Required Permissions & Why

SnipScreen requests the minimum permissions necessary:

  1. activeTab: Allows capturing the content of the tab you are currently viewing *only when you click the extension icon*.
  2. storage: Necessary for saving your settings (sync) and temporarily holding the screenshot image during editing (local).
  3. downloads: Required for the "Save" button to initiate the file download to your computer.
  4. tabs: Needed to open the dedicated editor interface in a new browser tab after capture.
  5. notifications: Used to display brief confirmation or error messages (e.g., "Image Copied!", "Save Failed").
  6. clipboardWrite: Required specifically to allow the "Copy" button to place the captured/edited *image* data onto your clipboard.

Your Choices & Control

You maintain control over the Extension and its data:

  1. You can clear data associated with extensions, including settings stored by SnipScreen, using the "Clear Browse data" options within Chrome's settings.
  2. You can disable or completely remove the SnipScreen extension at any time from Chrome's extensions page (chrome://extensions).

Children's Privacy

SnipScreen is not directed at or intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age depending on jurisdiction).

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes occur, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
