Key Benefits

Easy Capture

Instantly capture your visible tab with a simple click.

Quick Editing

Crop, blackout, add text, and draw arrows in a clean editor.

Simple Sharing

Copy to clipboard or save as a high-quality PNG with one click.

Privacy First

100% local processing. Your sensitive screenshots never leave your computer.

Simple Steps

1. Capture

Click the SnipScreen icon. (Single-click for quick crop, Double-click for full editor).

2. Edit

Use the clean toolbar in the new editor tab to Crop, Blackout, add Text, or draw Arrows.

3. Finish

Click 'Copy' to grab the edited image instantly, or 'Save' to download it as a PNG file.
